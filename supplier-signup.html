How It Works

  1. 1
    Register Your Mine or Quarry
    Complete the registration form below with your operation details, available materials, and TxDOT certifications. We verify your credentials within 48 hours.
  2. 2
    Authorize H. H. Holdings as Contractor
    Sign a simple contractor agreement allowing us to represent your materials in county government bids. You retain full control over pricing and availability.
  3. 3
    List Your Materials
    We add your aggregates, gravel, limestone, and other materials to our catalog with live pricing. County procurement offices see your products immediately.
  4. 4
    Receive Orders & Fulfill
    When counties place orders, we handle contracts and invoicing. You receive order confirmations, fulfill directly to the project site, and get paid promptly upon delivery.
